# Competitive brief — LINE MINI Eats (direct competitor)

_Research-synthesis, June 2026. Sources: lineforbusiness.com/th/service/lineminieats, Thairath, search._

## What it is
LINE's own in-chat ordering + payment solution for restaurants — order & pay inside LINE, no app
download. Positioned as a digital storefront/management system; **0% GP (no per-order commission)**.

## Features (merchant)
- **Wongnai Mobile Order & Pay** — order reception, multi-location menu management, QR PromptPay.
- **MyCustomer | CRM** — customer data, point accumulation, **tiered membership (up to 5 levels)**,
  automatic POS integration.
- **Restaurant Homepage** — customizable branded storefront on LINE (location, menu, pricing,
  branding, **promotion scheduling**).
- Customer flow: search/scan → select → **pay upfront** → LINE notification → **pickup at store**
  ("รับที่ร้าน ไม่ต้องต่อคิว").
- Setup: 5 steps (register → connect LINE OA → pick package → configure via Wongnai Merchant App →
  customize).

## Pricing
- **฿1,390 / month / location** (promo through 30 Sep 2026). Multi-location → sales consult.
- 14-day free trial.

## Strategic read
LINE MINI Eats **neutralises two of KhaiDee's differentiators**: "0% commission" and "order via LINE"
— it has both, backed by LINE's distribution + Wongnai discovery (which KhaiDee cannot match).
**Do not compete on distribution or on "no-commission alone."**

## Where KhaiDee still wins (defensible vs LINE MINI Eats)
| Dimension | LINE MINI Eats | KhaiDee | Edge |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price | ฿1,390/mo/location | Free · Pro ฿299 · Founder ฿199 | **KhaiDee 4–5× cheaper** |
| Real queue system | pickup-notify only | queue board + voice call-out + queue-first + party size | KhaiDee |
| Self-serve setup | Wongnai app + sales consult | sign up in 1 min + sample menus | KhaiDee (long-tail/street) |
| Works without LINE | no (LINE-centric) | counter-only POS works standalone | KhaiDee |
| Ecosystem independence | tied to LINE/Wongnai | owned by the shop | KhaiDee |
| Distribution / discovery | huge (native on LINE) | none | **LINE MINI Eats** |
| 5-tier membership / CRM | full | stamps + badge tiers (close) | tie |

## Adopt / improve (product backlog from this study)
1. **Promo scheduling + LINE broadcast to existing customers** — gap; they have promo scheduling.
   High value: push offers to the owned customer base (re-engagement). 
2. **Formalise membership tiers** — we have loyalty stamps + badge tiers; package them as a
   configurable membership program with per-tier perks.
3. **Branded "shop homepage"** in the LIFF — we have theming; elevate to a polished shop profile page.

## Positioning shift (decision)
"ไม่หักคอม" is no longer the lead (LINE doesn't charge GP either). New lead vs LINE MINI Eats:
**"ระบบคิวจริง + ราคาเริ่มฟรี + เปิดเองใน 1 นาที + ใช้ได้แม้ไม่มี LINE"** — target the micro / street /
drinks shops for whom ฿1,390/mo is too much and who don't need the Wongnai ecosystem. "No commission +
own your customer" still leads **vs delivery marketplaces (Grab / LINE MAN)**, a separate competitor set.
